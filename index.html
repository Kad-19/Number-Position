Instructions

  • The basic idea of this game is to correctly guess a hidden 4 digit number.
  • At every attempt the computer gives back the amount of the numbers correctly guessed and how many of them are in correct position
  • "Number" represents the amount of correctly guessed numbers and "Position" represents the amount of numbers correct in position
    For example: Let say your guess number is '1234' and the hidden number happens to be '8243'
    The computer gives back "Number" value '3' because the numbers'234' are found both in the hidden number and your guess
    And it gives back "Position" value '1' because only the number'2' is correctly guessed in exact position.
  • Easy level grants 10 attempts to guess the hidden number
  • Medium level grants 7 attempts to guess the hidden number
  • Hard level grants 4 attempts to guess the hidden number
  • You can win the game by correctly guessing the exact hidden number before you run Out of attempts
